Trending cryptocurrencies

Bitcoin is the pioneering cryptocurrency, created in 2009, designed for decentralized peer-to-peer transactions with a capped supply of 21 million coins.

Shiba Inu is a meme-based cryptocurrency with a Shiba Inu dog as its mascot, gaining attention for its ShibaSwap decentralized exchange and community-driven initiatives.

Solana is a high-performance blockchain network known for its fast transaction speeds and low fees, facilitating decentralized applications and smart contracts.

Ethereum is a leading blockchain platform enabling decentralized applications and smart contracts, powered by its native cryptocurrency Ether (ETH).
